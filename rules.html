1.Submission of Grievances

Students are required to submit their grievances through the designated online portal. Anonymous grievances will not be entertained.

2.Grievance Review Process

Each grievance will be reviewed by the Grievance Redressal Committee within 5 business days of submission. The committee will assess the nature of the grievance and take appropriate action.

3.Confidentiality

All information related to grievances will be kept confidential and will only be accessible to authorized personnel involved in the grievance redressal process.

4.Appeals

If a student is not satisfied with the outcome of the initial review, they may file an appeal within 10 business days. The appeal will be reviewed by a higher authority.

5.Non-Discrimination

The grievance redressal process will be conducted without any form of discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or any other factors.

6.Compliance with Policies

Students are expected to comply with all policies and procedures outlined by the institution regarding the grievance redressal process.
