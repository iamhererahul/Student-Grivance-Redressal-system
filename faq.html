Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I submit a grievance?
A: You can submit a grievance by logging into your account and using the "Submit Grievance" option in the dashboard.
Q: How long does it take to process a grievance?
A: The processing time for a grievance may vary depending on the nature of the grievance. Typically, it takes between 5 to 10 business days.
Q: Can I track the status of my grievance?
A: Yes, you can track the status of your grievance by logging into your account and checking the status in the dashboard.
